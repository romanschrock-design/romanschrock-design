Event Overview

Get ready for a day filled with delicious ribs, live music, and fun for the whole family! Here’s what you need to know to make the most of this amazing event:

Public Schedule

  • 12:00 PM – Doors open to the public – live music, vendors, and more!
  • 1:00 PM – Enjoy The Local Yokels on the Winery Stage
  • 2:00 PM – Rib tasting begins – try some of the best ribs around
  • 3:30 PM – Awards ceremony – find out who’s crowned rib champion!
  • 6:30 PM - 9:00 PM – Closing out the night with Hipper than Hip on the Main Stage

  • 5:00 PM – Wrap-up and cleanup
